This identity for the City of Melbourne really caught my eye. More often than not, the brand identity for a city can end up looking dull and contrived, sometimes even too corporate. This identity is essentially for Melbourne’s council, but doesn’t look like a ‘typically boring’ council…

This is great stuff.  Doesn’t look municipal or governmental at all.  Super well done Melbourne.
