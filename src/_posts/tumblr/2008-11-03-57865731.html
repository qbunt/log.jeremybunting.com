I don’t care who you vote for.  Just please, for the sake of the country you live in, go out and vote.  There’s no reason not to, it’s not hard, and it helps preserve the foundations this country was founded on. Your vote counts, no matter how much you don’t think it will.

Just so you know, I’ve never really been involved in politics, never openly said anything publicly about politics.  Make your voice heard. VOTE dammit.
